What? Why?

A waffle iron is two pans with interior waffle-patterned insides to give the waffle it's shape while it bakes the batter into. Waffles are the most valuable part of any breakfast, and in order to make them, a waffle iron is required. Waffle irons have been used for years in order to make the perfect waffle everyone enjoys. Waffles can be made into a variety of shapes which all revolves around the waffle iron that is used. Waffles are a common thing in many people's homes, but very few actually know much about a waffle iron and it's history.
