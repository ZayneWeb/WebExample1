Who Am I

They call me Zayne! I am a waffle enthusiast of many years and I made this site to inform the newcomers of waffles and the magnificent structure used to make them. I have for many years enjoyed waffles and never understood why or how they were made until recently, and I wanted to inform others of the magnificent information of what we know to be a waffle. Waffles have a great taste that many know and enjoy but few can tell the actual difference between the waffle and the pancake except the texture and looks. What actually differentiates them though? I am here to answer these myths and give information for all to enjoy and walk away with valuable information to pass to future generations!
